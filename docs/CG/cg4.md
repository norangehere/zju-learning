# Basic Math

## Vector

- multiplication, normalization
- addition(for any dimension)
- dot product: directional projection of $a$ onto $b$ ($b$ being a unit vector)

- orthonormal vectors: perpendicular and unit length
  - 傅里叶变换中的基函数也是正交基

- cross product

## Line Equation
